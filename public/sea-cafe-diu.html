 Our Story

Where Every Meal Feels Like
a Celebration of Flavour

Nestled in the heart of the beautiful Union Territory of Diu, Bavarchi - The Indian Cuisine Restaurant is more than a restaurant — it's a culinary landmark. Born from a passion for fresh ingredients and warm hospitality, we have been welcoming families, tourists, and food lovers from across India and beyond.

From our aromatic paneer preparations to fragrant Indian curries, crispy Chinese starters, and refreshing beverages — every plate tells a story of love, tradition, and pure vegetarian flavour. Our warm ambiance, hygienic open kitchen, and attentive service make us the go-to pure veg dining destination in Diu. We also feature a grand party and event hall that can accommodate over 900 guests — perfect for weddings, receptions, and celebrations of every kind.

Party & Event Hall
for 900+ Guests

Planning a grand celebration? Bavarchi offers one of the largest pure vegetarian party halls in Diu, capable of hosting over 900 guests. Whether it is a wedding reception, engagement ceremony, birthday bash, corporate event, or a festive gathering — our spacious and elegant hall is the perfect venue for your special occasion.

Our dedicated events team ensures every detail is taken care of — from customized pure veg menus crafted by our expert chefs to elegant decor, professional sound systems, and seamless service. With ample parking, hygienic kitchen facilities, and a warm ambiance, your guests will leave with unforgettable memories.

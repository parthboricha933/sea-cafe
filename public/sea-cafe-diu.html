 Our Story

Where Every Meal Feels Like
a Seaside Getaway

Nestled in the heart of the beautiful Union Territory of Diu, Sea Cafe & Food Destination is more than a restaurant — it's a culinary landmark. Born from a passion for fresh ingredients and warm hospitality, we have been welcoming families, tourists, and food lovers from across India and beyond.

From our freshly-caught seafood specials to fragrant Indian curries, crispy fast food bites, and refreshing beverages — every plate tells a story of love, tradition, and coastal flavour. Our relaxed beachside ambiance, hygienic open kitchen, and attentive service make us the go-to dining destination in Diu.
